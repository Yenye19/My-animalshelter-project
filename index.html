    Considering adding a new pet to your family?Great!!For full options details on our process, read the 'Steps to Adopt' page below.

    Did you know you can view all our available pets online?

    Our Patengo page is updated in real time,so as soon as a pet is available for

    adoption they will show up on the webpage.And as soon as they are adopted,

    their photos and bio will disappear.You can view all our available dogs, cats, rabbits

    guinea pigs, and more at the link below!

    Curious about how to go about adopting a

    new pet from our shelter? it's pretty straightforward!

    Please review our new virtual adoption process steps for all the details!

    and range from $5(small animals like mice and hamsters)

    to $200 (puppies under six months of age).
